Discuss the scope of var, let, and const

var and let and const is the keywords to declare a variable in the javascript.

  • 1. var is a function/global scoped and it can be redeclare and it can be reassigned
  • 2. let is a block scoped and it can't be redeclare but can be reassigned
  • 3. const is a block scoped and it can't be redeclare and can't be reassigned
